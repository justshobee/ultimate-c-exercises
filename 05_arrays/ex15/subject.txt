Exercise 15: Merge Two Arrays
Practice Problem: Write a C program that takes two separate integer arrays, A and B, and merges all their elements into a third, larger array, C. Print the resulting merged array.

Given:

int arrA[] = {10, 20, 30};
int arrB[] = {40, 50, 60, 70};
Expected Output:

Merged array C: 10 20 30 40 50 60 70
