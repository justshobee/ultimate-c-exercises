Exercise 26: Separate Even and Odd Numbers

Practice Problem: Write a C program that takes one integer array and separates its elements into two new arrays: one containing all the even numbers and the other containing all the odd numbers. Print all three arrays.

Given:

int arr[] = {10, 15, 22, 27, 34, 41, 50};

Expected Output:

Even array: 10 22 34 50
Odd array: 15 27 41
