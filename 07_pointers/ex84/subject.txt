Exercise 14: Character Frequency
Practice Problem: Calculate and print the frequency (count) of every lowercase alphabet character (‘a’ through ‘z’) present in a given string

Given:

char str[] = "Jessa";
int freq[26] = {0}; // Initialize all 26 counters to zero
Expected Output:

Character Frequencies (a-z):
'a': 1
'e': 1
'j': 1
's': 2
