Exercise 10: Sum of Positive and Negative Numbers Separately

Practice Problem: Write a C program that iterates through an array of positive, negative, and zero integers and calculates two separate sums: the total sum of all positive numbers, and the total sum of all negative numbers.

Given:

int size = 9;
int arr[9] = {15, -5, 20, 0, -10, 30, -25, 5, 0};

Expected Output:

Array elements: 15, -5, 20, 0, -10, 30, -25, 5, 0
Sum of all positive numbers: 70
Sum of all negative numbers: -40
