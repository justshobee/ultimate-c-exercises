Exercise 4: Count Even and Odd Elements

Practice Problem: Write a C program that counts and print the total number of even numbers and the total number of odd numbers stored within an integer array.

Given:

int arr[8] = {1, 4, 7, 12, 15, 20, 3, 10};

Expected Output:

Array: 1, 4, 7, 12, 15, 20, 3, 10
Total even elements: 4
Total odd elements: 4
