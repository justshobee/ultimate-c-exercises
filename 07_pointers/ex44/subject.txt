Exercise 14: Print Unique Elements

Practice Problem: Develop a C program that initializes an integer array and identifies and prints all unique elements (those that appear exactly once in the array).

Given:

int arr[] = {5, 2, 8, 5, 1, 2, 9, 8, 4};

Expected Output:

Array: 5 2 8 5 1 2 9 8 4 
Unique elements are: 1 9 4
