Exercise 25: Check Anagrams
Practice Problem: Determine if two strings are anagrams of each other (meaning they contain the exact same characters with the same frequencies, regardless of order). Case and spaces should be ignored for a robust check.

Given:

char str1[] = "listen";
char str2[] = "silent";
Expected Output:

String 1: listen
String 2: silent
Result: They are anagrams.
