Exercise 3: Arithmetic Operations

Practice Problem: Write a C program that calculates and prints the sum, difference, product, and quotient (division) of two integers.

Given:

int a, b;
a = 10;
b = 20;

Expected Output:

Sum: 20 + 10 = 30
Difference: 20 - 10 = 10
Product: 20 * 10 = 200
Quotient: 20 / 10 = 2.00
